About

Background

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Chemistry at Caltech, working with Professors K. N. Houk and W. A. Goddard III on computational and experimental problems in catalysis and materials.

My research combines DFT-level computation with wet-lab synthesis to uncover mechanisms, design new catalysts, and build predictive models for selectivity and reactivity.

Prior to Caltech, I earned my BSc summa cum laude from USTC, where I was ranked in the top 2% nationally and received the Guo Moruo Scholarship — the highest honor for graduating students.
